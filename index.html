WELCOME TO MY SITE

"Hi, I'm Izaias, a junior researcher in Geography.
I'm currently doing my PhD in Environmental Analysis and Geographic Information Processing."

I have experience in REMOTE SENSING DATA PROGRAMMING LAND USE AND LAND COVER GIS SPATIAL ANALYSIS

Some things about me.

I am the only child of family farmers from the interior of the state of Bahia, in the northeast of Brazil. I had all my schooling in public institutions and financed by public funds.

In my initial scientific training (Degree in Geography/2016-2020), I addressed the advance of the agricultural frontier in the Cerrado Domains of Mato Grosso and its socio-environmental implications.

Later, I immersed myself in the field of Digital Processing of Remote Sensing Data (Master's Degree in Geography/2020-2022), where I presented a proposal for mapping land cover and land use using the Progressive Detailing Method.

Between 2022 and 2023, I was a Development and Research Fellow at the National Institute for Space Research (INPE), as part of the Brazilian Biomes Mapping and Monitoring Project. Since then, I have had intense interaction with researchers from Brazil and abroad, allowing me to become familiar with multiple methods and techniques for processing remote sensing data.

At the end of 2023, I received the Ailton Luchiari Award for Geotechnologies at the XV National Meeting of Postgraduate Studies and Research in Geography. At the beginning of 2024, I was awarded outstanding author at the I PPGCIAMB Week: I Summer Course and X Seminar, at the Federal University of Goiás.

I'm currently developing my PhD thesis in Geography at the Federal University of Goiás (UFG) with a sandwich period at the National Institute for Space Research (INPE), where I'm improving the proposal for mapping land cover and land use using the Progressive Detailing Method.

I'm a musician (violinist), I like birds and I have great admiration for Ariano Suassuna and Ayrton Senna.
